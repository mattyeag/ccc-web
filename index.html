About Christ's Covenant Church

Christ's Covenant Church is a welcoming community dedicated to faith, worship, and service. We believe in building relationships, growing in faith, and making a positive impact in our community. All are welcome — just an open heart and a desire to connect.

Ideal for families, individuals, and seekers looking for a place to belong. Whether you're new to faith or seeking deeper connection, our church offers a supportive environment for spiritual growth.

Church Government

We believe that we are an independent local church. We humbly acknowledge our Lord Jesus Christ as our only Sovereign Head and accept the Bible to be the true center of Christian union.

We believe in the universal church, made up of those who have become genuine followers of Jesus Christ and have personally appropriated the gospel. We believe the Church exists to worship and glorify God as Father, Son and Holy Spirit; that it also exists to serve him by faithfully doing his will in the earth; that this involves a commitment to see the gospel preached and churches planted in the entire world for a testimony; that the ultimate mission of the Church is worship and the means by which this is accomplished is the making of disciples through the preaching and embracing of the gospel. We believe that when God transforms human nature, this then becomes the chief means of society's transformation.

We believe all members of the Church universal are to be a vital and committed part of a local church; that in this context, they are called to walk out the New Covenant as the people of God and demonstrate the reality of the kingdom of God; that the ascended Christ has given gift ministries to the church (including apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors and teachers) for the equipping of Christ's body that it might mature and grow, and that through the gift ministries, all members of the church are to be restored and equipped for the work of the ministry, employing their God-given gifts in His service in relation to one another and to the world.

We believe that we are an independent local church.

We believe in the New Testament structure of local church government made up as elders with the help of deacons. We adhere to the qualifications set down in the Holy Scriptures.

We believe that men called by God to the offices of apostle; prophet, evangelist, pastor and teacher will function in the body, "For the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ." This will be done in harmony with the local elders and deacons who make up the local government.
